Tools I Use

Software

Sketch

Sketch is a great program for UI Design. Even though it’s a new software, it sneaked into my workflow really fast. It’s also great for Visual Design, as it has a lot of effects like multiple shadows and blurs.
Download Sketch

Hardware

Wacom Intuos Pro Medium

I just got this pen tablet, but it’s an instant booster on my workflow. You can set up the pen’s buttons to trigger your most used commands, increasing productivity. Also the buttons on the tablet let you have everything within reach.
Buy Wacom Intuos Pro

Paper

RHODIA dotPad

I simply love this sketchbook. It’s simply amazing. The dots serve as layout but are unobstrusive on plain sight. I use it for wireframes and some low-fi mockups.
Buy Rhodia dotPad

Pen

Sakura Pigma Microns

These pens come in all shapes and sizes, and each one of them is fantastic. The ink is great and doesn’t smudge, that’s why I use them to mockup on my Rhodia dotPad. I use the 03 (0.35mm) most of the time.
Buy Sakura Pigma Microns

Markers

Copic Markers

Copic markers are great for adding depth to mockups. I mainly use grey markers, along with a red and blue one, to make some notes.
Buy Copic Markers
