I have a general design process.
Check it out!

Want to work together? Great, I'd love to work with you. However, before we get started, I want to be clear about how I work, what I expect of you and whay you can expect from me.
This is a general process, and will be adapted to fit your project. This project-specific process will be delivered to you before the project starts.

You can learn more about my process here.

My Process

  1. INITIAL CONTACT: QUESTIONNAIRE

    2. I have a questionnarie designed to determine the fit between us. This questionnaire also serves me to know more about the project needs and objectives. Your answers will also help me create a quote.

  2. OBJECTIVES REFINEMENT

    3. If we are a good fit, we will continue to talk about the project. This helps me refine the project goals and objectives. I will then send you an email with the project-specific process and the quote.

  3. TIMES, QUOTE, AND DOWN PAYMENT

    4. After I’ve sent you the specific process, and you agree to work under it, you’ll have to make the down payment discussed in the quote. All of the payment dates will be detailed in the process sent to you. I will also explain all of the project due dates and what the deliverables are.

  4. CONTENT DELIVERY

    5. For me to design the User Interface, I will need all the content that will go in it. If you don’t have the content already, or need to update it to better target your market, I can help you get a copywriter. Under no conditions will I design without the content.

  5. USER RESEARCH AND USER STORY

    6. After I receive all of the content, I will do a user research so I can design to your users best needs. My goal here is to align your users and your business objectives. A user story will be created to start planning the information architecture.

  6. WIREFRAMES, MOCKUPS AND PROTOTYPES

    7. This part of the process is a non-linear one. I will iterate between doing Wireframes, Mockups and Prototyoes, and testing them with the users. Keep in mind that all changes will be done to satisfy your clients needs, and will be objective decisions. Your personal preferences won’t be taken into account, to ensure the sucess of the design.

  7. USER INTERFACE

    8. This is the final step. I will design the final User Interface. Depending on the project, I can work with the developers to make the transition from Design to Code as easy as possible. I have Frontend coding experience, but please be aware that I will only help the development team, and not code the User Interface myself.
